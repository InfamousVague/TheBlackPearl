Questions

Good to know.

Is GhostWire free?

Yes — GhostWire is free to download and use, with no account and no sign-in.

Does GhostWire host or provide any content?

No. GhostWire hosts, stores, and indexes no media of its own. Every result comes from third-party sources that you choose to configure — you decide what it searches.

Do I need API keys for posters and metadata?

No. Artwork and metadata work out of the box — covers are fetched and cached for you. Advanced users can still plug in their own keys if they prefer.

Which platforms are supported?

macOS (Apple Silicon & Intel), Windows, and Linux, with native desktop builds for each.

How do updates work?

GhostWire ships with a built-in auto-updater, so you stay on the latest version automatically.

Help seed GhostWire

GhostWire distributes its own builds over BitTorrent. Grab the latest release as a magnet and seed it to help others download faster — or just turn on “Download updates over BitTorrent” in the app. Every build is verified against the same signature before it installs.
